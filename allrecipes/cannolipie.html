Cannoli pie recipe :


Ingredients required :

  • 1 (9-inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar, 1 1/2 cups full fat ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped lightly salted pistachios
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips, plus more for topping

Steps to follow :

Step 1 : Gather all ingredients, and preheat the oven to 325 degrees F(165 degrees C).

Step 2 : Bake graham cracker crust in the preheated oven until golden brown and fragrant, about 10 minutes. Let cool completely on a wire rack.

Step 3 : Add heavy whipping cream to a clean bowl, and beat with an electric mixer to stiff peaks. Set aside.

Step 4 : Beat cream cheese and powdered sugar together in another bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
